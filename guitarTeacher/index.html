Our Goal

We believe that with better feedback and effective mentorship,
we can reduce confusion and frustration inherent in learning how to play the guitar.

How it all started ...

Need Finding

Although there are apps that let people learn how to play songs, we found that they were mostly focused on chord sheets. We created Guitar Teacher to help beginning guitar players connect with a mentor to learn from, and a community to spur them on. After logging in, users begin by selecting an instructor to contact. They can call or email the instructor through our app.

Personalization

Our app also allows users to edit their own profile, changing their user name, contact info, etc.
