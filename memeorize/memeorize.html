Memeorize

“I’m stressed out from studying… I need a break.” says a college student after studying for an hour. She pulls up her phone and scrolls down her Facebook newsfeed to procrastinate on looking at course materials. After five hours of mindless scrolling, she feels guilty and unproductive. Although memes are becoming more prominent in the technology age among students, why does memes have to be a distraction? We thought it would be helpful to combine entertainment with education where students could elaborate on academic materials with humor to increase memory retention and to bridge academics and social activities in their downtime.

  • Learn More

Problem Statement

K-12 & college students need an entertaining learning method where they could actively apply connections on course concepts to relatable situations to increase engagement for deeper understanding, to encourage interaction with fellow peers, and to alleviate dullness from just pure memorization.
